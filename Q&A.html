    These are couple of questions about our school, filming, teachers, etc.

    Q1: What is this school all about?
    A: This school is all about filming your own film and Directing it or acting for it.
    Q2: Does this school include Animation?
    A: No it doesn't, but we have some animators that was in this school and graduated from the school to major Animation in College.
    Q3: How many teachers were hired to teach in the school (2019 - 2020)?
    A: About Seven New Teachers.
    Q4: Are there any bullies in the School?
    A: No, we do not have any bullies.
    Q5: Does this school have an Instagram account?
    A: Yes, our instagram is @actvfpta and it is monitored by the PTA.
    Q6: Does this school include any Audition/Presentation?
    A: Not Like any other high schools, there are no audition or presentation, just put Actvf as first option and you could be accepted.
    Q7: What is the school mascot?
    A: Yes, our mascot is Panthers.
    Q8: Where is the school located?
    A: The school is located in Long Island City 11101. (Address: 1-50 51st Ave)
    Q9: How can my child travel to the school?

    A: He/she can take the seven train to Vernon blvd-Jackson Avenue or take the G Train to 21st Street. For Metro Buses, you can take Q103 to Vernon Blvd/Borden Ave or take B62 to 11th Street/Jackson Ave. If you live near the school, you can just walk to the entrance that is located at 51st Avenue between 2nd Street and Center Boulevard.

    Q10: Will my child have an email for this school?

    A: Yes, he/she will have an email with the last four digit of her/his student id and it will end with @ and school website url, actvf.org. (Example: dpala9249@actvf.org, the student name is Dayanna Pala as it generates as a name.)
