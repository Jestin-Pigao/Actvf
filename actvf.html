    WELCOME TO ACTVF 2020 WEBSITE!!!





    School

    Actvf is a high school and it is home to 9th graders through 12th graders. This school includes programs like Cohorts, Electives, and Film. This school was founded in 2008. Now, it is sitting in Hunters Point Campus, shared with two schools and it sits in 4th and 5th floor.

    The Balcony

    After moving the school from the basement, the school is in a middle school building that also includes the balcony and your welcome to enjoy the outside, but even if its gonna rain, snow, or any other weather condition, the balcony will be closed for the whole lunch period to keep you safe. Even though, the balcony is wet, you will still not be allowed outside. It also connects to the big cafeteria and small cafeteria.

    The Cafeteria

    Welcome to the Cafeteria, its a place where you can eat breakfast and lunch with your friends. Unlike Elementary School, Middle School, Private School, and others, you are allowed to use your phones, ipad, or any electronic device, but you are not allowed to use speakers and play loud music, it's against the cafeteria rules, this means that you have to listen to them with headphones or earbuds or anytype of hearing. This cafeteria can also include school dances, events, after-school activities, etc. There are two cafeteria's. One is the small cafeteria and the second one is the big cafeteria. Also, this includes a vending machine to get any snacks, but there's no drinks.
